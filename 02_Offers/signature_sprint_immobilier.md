# Signature Sprint Immobilier

## Role

Main active offer direction for TimDesign.

## Promise

A premium Webflow site or microsite for a real estate project, delivered quickly and designed to improve perceived value, clarity, and lead quality.

## Target

- real estate developers in French-speaking Switzerland;
- premium agencies and brokers;
- architect-promoters;
- commercial teams launching a high-value property/project.

## Problem

The project may be architecturally strong, visually refined, or commercially valuable, but its website does not communicate that value. Generic pages, weak UX, slow sites, or poor storytelling lower trust and attract less qualified interest.

## Solution

A focused sprint that frames the commercial message, structures the page/site, creates a premium visual direction, and builds it in Webflow.

## Value

- improves perceived value;
- clarifies the offer;
- gives sales teams a stronger presentation asset;
- supports pre-commercialization;
- avoids the heaviness of a large agency;
- creates a reusable proof asset for TimDesign.

## Scope Guardrails

Core:

- strategic framing;
- UX/content structure;
- premium visual design;
- Webflow development;
- responsive QA;
- launch support.

Not included by default:

- full brand identity;
- 3D production;
- CRM system build;
- complex automation;
- large content production;
- financial performance guarantees.

## Packaging Direction

Keep packaging simple:

- Essential: premium landing page for one project.
- Premium: richer microsite with project story, location, gallery, contact.
- Custom: higher-end visual/motion system for iconic projects.

Use exact pricing only when confirmed.
