# TimDesign Positioning

## Core Position

TimDesign creates premium Webflow websites for demanding real estate projects and adjacent premium service businesses in French-speaking Switzerland.

The core business concept is perception: the digital presentation of a high-value project should match the real quality and ambition of the project.

## Short Formula

Premium Webflow sites for real estate projects that need image, clarity, and commercial credibility.

## Promise

Create a refined, credible, commercially clear digital presentation that raises perceived value, supports trust, and makes contact easier.

## Target

- real estate developers;
- premium agencies and brokers;
- architect-promoters;
- design/architecture studios involved in property commercialization;
- premium service businesses whose website weakens perceived value.

## Problem

Many ambitious projects are presented through generic, slow, visually weak, or poorly structured websites. This creates a perception gap: the offer may be valuable, but the digital experience makes it feel less credible.

## TimDesign Difference

TimDesign is the boutique alternative between a heavy agency and a generic freelancer:

- premium execution without agency heaviness;
- direct expert relationship;
- strong visual and UX taste;
- Webflow delivery speed;
- commercial clarity, not decoration for its own sake.

## Current Messaging

Use:

- presentation haut de gamme;
- quality of perception;
- commercial clarity;
- support de commercialisation;
- direct expert execution;
- boutique Webflow studio;
- value perception;
- refined digital experience.

Avoid:

- ultra-luxury caricature;
- HNWI/UHNWI language in sales copy;
- exaggerated ROI promises;
- low-cost positioning;
- selling TimDesign as a full-service 360 agency.

## Website Direction

The TimDesign site should communicate maturity, precision, and premium real estate relevance.

Useful homepage flow:

1. Hero: the project deserves better than a generic online presence.
2. Problem: poor presentation weakens perceived value.
3. Solution: a digital sales support built for perception and clarity.
4. Offer: strategy, visual direction, Webflow build.
5. Proof: selected projects, concepts, or demonstrations.
6. Difference: direct expert execution.
7. Process: framing, structure, design, Webflow, launch.
8. CTA: discuss whether the current digital presentation matches the project value.
