# Prospection Operating Loop

## Principle

Notion is the live tracker for prospects, contacts, statuses, follow-ups, and interaction history.

TimDesign_OS stores reusable standards: positioning, offers, templates, qualification logic, and durable sales assets.

Do not rebuild a CRM in the OS.

## Basic Flow

1. Find or receive prospect.
2. Check Notion for existing history.
3. Qualify fit against TimDesign positioning and active offers.
4. Create a short prospect brief if useful.
5. Write a personalized message based on the prospect's actual site/context.
6. Log follow-up and status in Notion.
7. Promote only reusable learnings back into the OS.

## Active Angles

### Premium Real Estate Design

Use when the prospect's digital presence under-represents the quality of the project, agency, or property.

Focus on:

- perceived value;
- credibility;
- clarity;
- premium presentation;
- Webflow speed and maintainability.

### Cookie/Compliance

Use when cookie banners or consent setup visibly damage trust, UX, or legal confidence.

Keep it narrow. Do not let it replace the main premium Webflow positioning.

## Rules

- Never invent relationship history.
- Do not mass-mail generic copy.
- Do not mention personal details unless they directly strengthen the message.
- Do not write prospect-specific files into the OS unless they become reusable proof, client material, or validated strategy.
