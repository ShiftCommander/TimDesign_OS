# Proof Assets Protocol

## Purpose

Turn TimDesign projects, concepts, demos, and historical work into persuasive proof assets without inventing claims.

## Asset Types

- Case study: full proof story for website, proposal, or sales deck.
- Portfolio card: short visual proof for homepage or portfolio.
- Social post: one sharp angle for LinkedIn/Instagram.
- Private sales proof: restricted material for conversations or proposals.

## Rules

- Do not invent metrics.
- Do not invent testimonials.
- Label concepts clearly.
- Check permission before publishing real client work.
- Do not showcase old low-quality work as current premium positioning.

## Proof Criteria

A proof asset should show at least one of:

- premium visual execution;
- Webflow quality;
- better commercial clarity;
- stronger perceived value;
- delivery speed;
- UX improvement;
- client-relevant business result.

## Workflow

1. Start from a real project, concept, or demo.
2. Use `05_Templates/case_study_template.md`.
3. Keep claims factual.
4. Connect the proof to Signature Sprint or the relevant offer.
5. Store reusable proof here or in `04_Clients` if client-specific and durable.
